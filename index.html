About

Hi, I'm Izzy

I'm a multidisciplinary artist, art teacher and community practitioner based in West Yorkshire.

Artist

For me, art is a calming and grounding practice. I find so much joy in experimentation, so I always start my process by exploring the unique properties and possibilities of various materials. I often become completely absorbed, creating a large body of work in my chosen medium and then gradually refining select pieces until they capture the mood I aimed to achieve. Though much of my work features negative space and organic shapes, I have never felt it fits within a particular specialism or style. Instead, I am an art enthusiast through and through, always open to trying different methods and techniques until I have grasped them, before moving on to something new.

Art teacher

I have loads of experience with schools and young people.
